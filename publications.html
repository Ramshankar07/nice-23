Publications



All the accepted and presented papers in the ICCPC'22 will be included in the conference proceedings and will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore. Authors of accepted papers, or at least one of them, are requested to register and present their work at the conference, otherwise their papers will not be included in the proceedings.
